Who will SURVIVE?

In this game, there are three players: 1.Human 2. Cockroach 3. Nuclear Bomb

When you click the play button, any two random no. shows up which resembles with the players as follows:

If the number when divided by 3, gives remainder 0 => Players will be Cockroach and Nuclear Bomb

If the number when divided by 3, gives remainder 1 => Players will be Human and Nuclear Bomb

If the number when divided by 3, gives remainder 2 => Players will be Cockroach and Human
